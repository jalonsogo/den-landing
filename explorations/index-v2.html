Why den

How it works

Three steps to hands-off.

STEP 01
Spin up a sandbox
Pick an agent, a base image and a workspace. den boots a fresh, disposable microVM in seconds.
STEP 02
Set the guardrails
Lock the network to the domains you trust, clone-isolate your repo, layer in your tools with kits and mixins.
STEP 03
Turn off the wheel
Flip on bypass-permissions and walk away. The agent runs full YOLO — sealed inside a box it can't escape.

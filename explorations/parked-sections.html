The idea

Wild on the inside.
Quiet on the outside.

A burrow of its own

Each agent settles into a disposable microVM with its own Docker daemon, filesystem and kernel. Nothing it touches is yours.

A guarded doorway

Nothing leaves without your say. Requests pass by the domains you allow; the rest are turned away at the door, and logged.

No more babysitting

Say yes once, then leave it be. The agent installs, builds and rewrites code on its own, and you come back to a finished diff.
